Project information

  • Steam Page: Invicta: The Next Queen

Description

INVICTA: The Next Queen is a TopDown Soulslike game where a legendary warrior must fight her way through hell and claim her soul once more. Take the role of Moma as you explore the labyrinthian dungeons that make up the Circle of Greed and defeat King Midas in single combat.

Further Details

Invicta: The Next Queen is a game developed by students of ESAT Valencia.
This group of students was conformed by artists, programmers and designers.

It was released on Steam on July 2023.

What I did

  • Character movement and control
  • Camera (including transitions)
  • UI
  • AI
  • Dash mechanic and physics
  • Responsible of ensuring good performance and optimization
  • Various minor features, like keys that follow the player

During the development of Invicta, I mainly focused on implementing the player movement mechanics.
This includes, moving, dashing, camera and changing between scenes. However, once I finished with that,
I was responsible for the UI of the game, as well as bugfixing and optimization.
During the final month of development, I worked on the AI of bosses and minor low priority features.
